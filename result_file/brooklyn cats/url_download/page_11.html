Donate

We can’t do it alone, we need your help! Brooklyn Animal Action is an all-volunteer, nonprofit 501(c)3 organization. We are small and efficient. All donations go directly to helping animals in need. Thank you for supporting the rescue, spay / neuter, and rehoming of cats in Brooklyn!

 

Make a one-time or recurring monthly donation on PayPal!





You can also donate by check, made out to Brooklyn Animal Action and sent to:

Brooklyn Animal Action
285 5th Avenue, PMB #456
Brooklyn, NY 11215

[Please note: This is a PO Box, not an actual office or shelter. BAA doesn’t have a central shelter–our kitties are all in foster homes.]

  • $25 provides testing for 1 cat for FIV (feline immunodeficiency virus) and FeLV (feline leukemia), which helps us identify care needs and find the right home
  • $50 provides spaying/neutering for 1 cat, an essential tool in reducing the number of homeless cats on the street and euthanasia rates in shelters
  • $75 provides 1 basic visit to a veterinarian for 1 cat to ensure his or her good health, including vaccinations, FIV/FeLV testing, and treatment for fleas/worms
  • $100 provides a 1 month supply of flea or deworming medicine for BAA’s cats
  • $200 provides a 1 week supply of food for BAA’s cats in foster homes

 

There’s more than one way to support BAA!

You can show your support for BAA’s rescue, adoption, and TNR programs just by shopping online. Check out the options below, and remember, BAA is entirely volunteer-run, so every dime donated goes directly to caring for animals in need.

 

Subscribe to KitNipBox

Imagine getting a box filled with new and unique kitty goodies delivered to your door every month! That’s exactly what you’ll get with KitNipBox – a monthly subscription service that handpicks healthy treats, fun toys, and much more for your kitties to enjoy. Use the special link above and BAA will receive a portion of the proceeds!

 

Shop via Amazon Smile

ow BAA can benefit every time you shop on Amazon! Simply go to Smile.Amazon.com — you’ll be prompted to select a charity on the first visit — and Amazon will donate a portion of your purchase to BAA. For twice the giving, use Smile.Amazon.com to donate something on BAA’s Amazon Wishlist, featured below!

 

Shop BAA’s Amazon Wishlist

Love Amazon? While you’re browsing the nonfiction bestsellers, add a fleece kitty bed or scratching post to your cart.

Shop via iGive.com

Get your favorite stores to donate to BAA! When you use iGive, online retailers will donate a percentage of your purchase. Retailers include Amazon, Best Buy, Staples, Petco, Crate and Barrel, Walgreens, Lowe’s, and many more.

 

Walk with ResQWalk!

 

Help animals one step at a time! Download the ResQWalk app and you can help raise money for Brooklyn Animal Action! You can earn prizes with each logged walk!

 

Shop BAA’s Petflow Wishlist

Help provide kitties in need with food, toys, and litterboxes. Plus, for every purchase, Petflow will donate an additional 10% to BAA!

 

Shop BAA Merch at CafePress (including our elusive dog logo!)

Show your BAA pride with an adorable tshirt, tote bag, sweatshirt, and more. Available in our classic cat logo and our harder-to-find dog logo!
