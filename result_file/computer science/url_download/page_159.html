Meet Our Faculty

Aaron Clauset
Assistant Professor

I care a lot about using computers to do better science. To give you a sense of what I mean, here is one of my favorite quotations, by the eminent physicist Paul Dirac: "In science one tries to tell people, in such a way as to be understood by everyone, something that no one ever knew before. But in poetry, it's the exact opposite."
