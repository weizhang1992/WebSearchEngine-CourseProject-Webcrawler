Computing (journal)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Computing, subtitled Archives for Scientific Computing, is a scientific journal published by Springer publishing research in computer science and numerical computation.[1] Its ISSN is 0010-485X for the print version and 1436-5057 for the electronic version. As of 2008[update], the editors of the journal are Hermann Brunner, Rainer Burkard, Craig Douglas, Wolfgang Hackbusch and Dietmar Saupe.[2] The first volume was published in 1966.
