  • Description: Constantius Gallus, Caesar. 351-354 AD. AV Solidus (4.44 gm). Thessalonica mint.
D N CONSTANTI-VS NOB CAES, bare-headed, draped, and cuirassed bust right
GLORIA REI-PVBLICAE, Roma and Constantinopolis enthroned, the former facing, the latter turned to the left, supporting between them a wreath inscribed VOT/V/MVLT/X in four lines; Roma holds spear, Constantinopolis holds sceptre and rests right foot on prow; *TES*.

RIC VIII 149; Depeyrot 8/4; Cohen 25.
