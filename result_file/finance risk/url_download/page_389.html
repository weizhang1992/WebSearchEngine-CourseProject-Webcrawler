    Global Maps of Political Risk

    Starting at $244.00
    The Global Maps of Political Risk provide quick visual indications of changes in political risk, based on each month’s issue of International Country Risk Guide (ICRG). All countries have been color-coded according to their current levels of overall risk (from ICRG’s Table 1), and each month the maps will also display unique icons denoting specific political risks (from ICRG Table 3B) in countries selected by the ICRG’s editorial staff. Each month the maps will highlight countries where the political risk situation is tenuous and likely to change over the near term. The map icons will focus on key countries for current international business decisions – based on the details discussed in the regional overviews and specific country analyses of that month’s issue of ICRG.
