Directions

POLY PREP'S MIDDLE SCHOOL, POLY PREP'S UPPER SCHOOL, ADMINISTRATION
Poly Prep Country Day School's main campus (including the Upper School, Middle School, and Administration) is located at the corner of Seventh Avenue and 92nd Street in Dyker Heights, Brooklyn.

Our Summer Camp also takes place on our Dyker Heights campus.

Our address is:

9216 Seventh Avenue
Brooklyn, New York 11228-3698
Phone: (718) 836-9800

The main entrance to our Dyker Heights' campus is on 92nd Street. The athletic fields are behind the school buildings, one-quarter mile south of the main entrance, on Seventh Avenue. Bus and van parking is also on Seventh Avenue. 

Click here for a Google map.

NOTE: Please allow for enough time when travelling by car or subway. All visitors must check in with security immediately upon arrival. 
POLY PREP'S LOWER SCHOOL
Poly's Lower School is located at 50 Prospect Park West on the corner of 1st Street in Park Slope, Brooklyn.

DRIVING & SUBWAY DIRECTIONS (Click on Expandable Links Below)
Middle/Upper School (Dyker Heights Campus)
POLY PREP SUMMER is located on this campus.

Poly Prep Country Day School is located at the corner of 7th Avenue and 92nd Street in Dyker Heights, Brooklyn. The main entrance is on 92nd Street, just west of Seventh Avenue. The athletic fields are behind the school, one-quarter mile south of the main entrance. Bus and van parking is on 7th Avenue. (Please allow enough time when traveling by subway or car)

  • By Subway (NYC subway maps are here)
The closest subway to Poly Prep is the R train (Queens Boulevard/Broadway/4th Avenue Local).

You can transfer to the R from:

- the F train at 4th Avenue/9th Street
- the D or N trains at 36th Street, the N train at 59th Street
- the 2, 3, or 4 train at either Borough Hall or Atlantic Avenue/Pacific Street

Take the R train to the last stop at 95th Street-Fort Hamilton in Bay Ridge. Walk past the last car on the train and exit the platform using the last stairway.  You will exit the subway on 4th Avenue.  Walk north to 92nd Street and turn right on that street. Walk about five blocks, crossing over the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway, to 7th Avenue. Poly's 92nd Street entrance will be on your right.

Note: Disruption of the R train service is expected until October 2014. There is no R train service between downtown Manhattan and Brooklyn. The R train will run in two segments (Manhattan/Queens and Brooklyn). 

  • By Car
From Points North (Bronx, Westchester, and Connecticut)

Take I-87 (if coming from the west) or I-95 and the Bruckner Expressway (if coming from the east) south to the Triborough Bridge. After crossing the bridge, keep to the right and, after about a mile, exit onto the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway (I-278). Take the BQE for thirteen miles, passing all of the Manhattan bridges and continuing south into Brooklyn. Stay to the right when the BQE divides (around 60th Street), and continue for another two miles, exiting at 92nd Street-Fort Hamilton Parkway (Exit 17), the last exit before the Verrazano Bridge. At the light at the top of the ramp, turn left onto 92nd Street. Take a right at the next traffic light for Poly's 92nd Street entrance.

From Manhattan

Take the Brooklyn Battery Tunnel and follow the signs for I-278 West. Stay to the right when the road divides (around 60th Street), and continue for another two miles, exiting at 92nd Street-Fort Hamilton Parkway (Exit 17), the last exit before the Verrazano Bridge. At the light at the top of the ramp, turn left onto 92nd Street. Take a right at the next traffic light for Poly's 92nd Street entrance.

From the Belt Parkway (Long Island)

Heading west, take Exit 4, 14th Avenue-Bay 8 Street. At the light, turn left onto Cropsey Avenue and go two blocks to 14th Avenue. Turn left and continue around the golf course for about one-quarter mile. Fourteenth Avenue runs into 7th Avenue. Continue up 7th Avenue to 92nd Street. Take a left on 92nd Street. Poly's 92nd Street entrance is on the left at the first traffic light.

From Northern New Jersey through Staten Island

Take I-95 South to the New Jersey Turnpike. Take Exit 13 to the Goethals Bridge. Cross into Staten Island and take I-278 East to the Verrazano Bridge (about seven miles). Cross the bridge and take the first exit, 92nd Street, Bay Ridge. At the light, turn right onto 92nd Street. Take a right at the next traffic light for Poly's 92nd Street entrance.

Lower School Campus (Park Slope)
  • By Subway (NYC subway maps are here)
F train to 7th Avenue

Take the F train to the 7th Avenue stop. You will exit the subway onto 9th Street at either 7th or 8th Avenue. Walk eight blocks to 1st Street. Make a right at 1st Street and walk up to Prospect Park West. Poly Prep Lower School is on the corner of 1st Street and Prospect Park West.

2 and 3 trains to Grand Army Plaza

Take the 2 or 3 train to the Grand Army Plaza stop. Proceed to Prospect Park West via Plaza Street West. Walk along Prospect Park West to 1st Street. Poly Prep Lower School is located on the corner of 1st Street and Prospect Park West.
  • By Bus
Nearby bus lines include:
- B69
- B41
- B67
  • By Car
From Manhattan

Take the Brooklyn Battery Tunnel and follow the signs for I-278 West. Stay to the left and merge onto the Prospect Expressway. Take Prospect Expressway to 10th Avenue–11th Avenue exit. Get off at 10th Avenue–11th Avenue exit. Go up the ramp to the stop sign. Go straight at the stop sign to the first light at 11th Avenue. Make a left onto 11th Avenue. Make a left at the first traffic light to get back on the Prospect Expressway going in the opposite direction. Get off at first exit 7th–8th Avenue. Make a right onto 8th Avenue. Go straight down 8th Avenue to 1st Street. Make a right onto 1st Street. Go up the block to the light at Prospect Park West. Poly Prep Lower School is on the corner of 1st Street and Prospect Park West.

From Brooklyn and Queens

Take the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway to the Prospect Expressway. Get on the Prospect Expressway. Take Prospect Expressway to 10th Avenu–11th Avenue exit. Get off at 10th Avenue–11th Avenue exit. Go up the ramp to the stop sign. Go straight at the stop sign to the first light at 11th Avenue. Make a left onto 11th Avenue. Make a left at the first traffic light to get back on the Prospect Expressway going in the opposite direction. Get off at first exit 7th–8th Avenue. Make a right onto 8th Avenue. Go straight down 8th Avenue to 1st Street. Make a right onto 1st Street. Go up the block to the light at Prospect Park West. Poly Prep Lower School is on the corner of 1st and Prospect Park West.

From Bay Ridge

Take 4th Avenue to 1st Street. Make a right onto 1st Street. Take 1st Street to the light at Prospect Park West. Poly Prep Lower School is on the corner of 1st Street and Prospect Park West.
