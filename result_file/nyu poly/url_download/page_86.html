Profile

Polytechnic Institute of New York University is a private research university located in Brooklyn, NY. Established in 1854 as Brooklyn Polytechnic Institute, it is the second-oldest private engineering school in the United States. In 2008 the school officially became affiliated with New York University under the name Polytechnic Institute of NYU. NYU-Poly is one of the ten schools in the NYU Entrepreneurs Network (NYUEN). Currently, NYU Poly has over 4,600 students who can select programs from 10 departments, including finance and risk engineering and technology management and innovation. Polytechnic Institute of New York University competes in NCAA Division III athletics, and its teams are known as the Fighting Blue Jays. More »
