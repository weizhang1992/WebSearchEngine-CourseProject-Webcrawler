Economic Advisors

Mike Metzger
CEO and President

Mike Metzger

A lifetime technology leader, Mike joined PayScale in 2004 as the Chief Executive Officer. Previously, he was the CEO and president of Performant, and also held product leadership positions at RealNetworks, and Microsoft. Mike holds an MBA in industrial administration from Carnegie Mellon University and a Bachelor of Science in mechanical engineering from Clarkson University.

Katie Bardaro
VP of Data Analytics

Katie Bardaro

Katie joined PayScale in 2008. In addition to leading the data analytics team and building and maintaining PayScale’s proprietary compensation model, Katie serves as PayScale’s lead economist. She has provided analysis on compensation data and trends for print/online and broadcast media including The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, Bloomberg Businessweek, The Economist, CNBC, CNN Money, USA Today, Forbes, and Business Insider, among others. Katie holds a bachelor's in economics from the College of the Holy Cross and a master's in economics from University of Washington, with a focus in labor economics and econometrics.

Ronni Bernhard
Controller

Ronni Bernhard

Ronni joined PayScale in 2014. Prior to joining PayScale she was the Director of Finance at All Star Directories. With more than 20 years' experience in financial and operational management she held Senior Financial Manager positions at both Nuance Communications and AOL in their digital services divisions. As a Controller she's worked the numbers side with a wide range of organizations, from publishing to tech start-ups to consultants in the health care industry. She has a BS in Business and graduated with honors from the University of Washington.

Melody Brown
Senior VP of Sales

Melody Brown

Coming to PayScale as a high achievement sales leader, Melody joined the company in 2014. With a depth of experience – from startups to Fortune 100 companies – her most recent role was VP Sales & Channel for DataCore Software. She also was Managing Principal & Owner at PEAK Consulting, and had several sales management positions at Lexmark. Melody serves our local community as a member on the Board of Directors of Pike Place Market Foundation.

Barnaby Dorfman
Chief Engineering Officer

Barnaby Dorfman

Barnaby joined PayScale in 2012 as general manager of the consumer product team. He has spent two decades managing large-scale Web products, including seven years at Amazon.com, where he was a vice president in the search technology group. He is a graduate of San Francisco State University and received his MBA from the Tuck School at Dartmouth College.

Preston Heffernan
VP of Business Development

He has a BS in Communications and Social Sciences from the University of Southern California.

Emily Jensen
VP of Sales

Emily Jensen

Emily Jensen, Vice President of Sales, joined PayScale in 2007 as an Account Executive. Emily was quickly promoted and began leading the western division of the PayScale sales team in 2009. Her leadership has been instrumental in PayScale's sales achievement, with the sales team posting its fastest growth to date under her direction. Prior to PayScale, Emily held roles in the art world, including work at the Eagle Gallery in London and the Seattle Art Museum. Emily is a graduate of the University of Colorado, Boulder. When not working, she enjoys spending time with her husband Drew, son Oliver and their two dogs. Emily is also passionate about supporting our local animal shelters and is currently serving as a foster parent for PAWS in Lynnwood.

Mark Klebanoff
Chief Financial Officer

Mark Klebanoff

To say Mark is a numbers guy is an understatement. Mark had more than 20 years of executive experience in finance and operations before he joined PayScale in 2014. Most recently, he was CFO at Appature and has also held CFO positions at All Star Directories, Daptiv, M:Metrics, AccessLine, RealNetworks and Industrial Systems. He earned a Bachelor's and a Master's of Business Administration degree from Yale University.

Stacey Klimek
VP of People

Stacey Klimek

Likely one of the most charismatic people in the office, Stacey moved into the head of human resources role in 2014, after joining PayScale in 2009 as the Controller. Stacey possesses more than 25 years of executive, financial, and administrative experience inside fast-growth enterprises, most recently as CFO of Redhawk Group. Previously, Stacey held leadership positions with NetMotion Wireless (acquired by Padcom Inc.) and Captura Software (acquired by Concur Technologies).
